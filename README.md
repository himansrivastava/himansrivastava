### Hi there 👋

I am Himanshu Srivastava, a software engineer by choice. I am an IIT graduate and have more than 11 years of industry experience.

- ⚡ My Story: I worked for more than 11 years for an Indian government company. Yes, it was a very stable job. But in last 1 year of the job I started questioning myself whether this is what I really wanted to do all my life. The answer was a resounding NO. All that I really wanted in life were two things: (1) A happy family. (2) To code and build something beautiful. I already had the first one. It was time to also focus on the second one. So, I finally decide to quit my job and focus full time on building my startup. Yes it was a tough decision but when your family shows confidence in you, you can dare to take such steps.
